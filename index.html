What we do

Technology-driven drone operations for real-world challenges

Altamira Drones is a Madrid-based drone company focused on delivering advanced aerial services for both urban and rural environments. We combine drone operations, visual intelligence, geospatial data and innovation-driven development to support technical inspections, photogrammetry, agricultural analysis, land management and next-generation digital solutions.

Our work goes beyond aerial imagery. We develop data-oriented and innovation-led services designed to support decision-making, operational efficiency and sector-specific applications in architecture, infrastructure, agriculture and livestock.

Urban Environments

High-precision drone services for the built environment, technical inspections, architecture, engineering and territorial analysis.

Agriculture & Livestock

Advanced aerial support for agricultural operations, spectral analysis, soil mapping, parcel management, livestock monitoring and precision decision support.

Innovation & R&D

A strong innovation profile with significant focus on R&D and the development of digital tools for agriculture, livestock and territorial intelligence.

01 / Urban Solutions

Aerial intelligence for the built environment

Altamira Drones provides advanced drone services for urban environments, buildings, technical assets, architecture, engineering and infrastructure-related operations. We help capture visual and geospatial information efficiently, safely and with a strong technical focus.

Technical Inspections

Drone-based visual and technical inspections for buildings, roofs, facades, structures and hard-to-access assets. Detailed observation, technical documentation and improved inspection workflows.

Photogrammetry

Aerial data capture for photogrammetric processing, spatial documentation and visual analysis of urban and built environments. Orthomosaics, 3D models and topographic support.

Building & Asset Observation

Targeted aerial observation of buildings, envelopes, structural surfaces and site conditions for technical review and operational support.

Construction Progress

Periodic aerial documentation for monitoring the evolution of projects, sites and interventions over time. Visual records for teams and stakeholders.

Architecture & Engineering

Drone services designed to support architects, technical consultants, engineers and planners with reliable visual and territorial information.

Site & Land Visualization

Aerial overviews of plots, developments, technical sites and urban land to improve understanding, planning and presentation.

Preventive Maintenance

Visual detection of apparent issues, surface anomalies and key points requiring closer technical assessment for maintenance planning.

Heritage & Complex Buildings

Support for the observation and documentation of architecturally complex, sensitive or hard-to-access structures and historical buildings.

We help urban and technical sectors work with better visibility, faster access to information and more efficient aerial documentation.

02 / Agriculture & Livestock

Precision aerial systems for land and livestock

Altamira Drones develops drone-based solutions for agriculture and livestock operations, combining aerial observation, territorial analysis, multispectral workflows and innovation-oriented tools to improve field management and operational decision-making.

Agriculture

Aerial Product Application

Operational aerial application services adapted to the needs and technical framework of each project, improving efficiency and coverage.

Multispectral Analysis

Generation and interpretation of aerial data using indexes such as NDVI, SAVI, TSAVI and other spectral indicators for vegetation and crop analysis.

Soil & Parcel Mapping

Drone-based mapping to support parcel organization, land analysis, zoning, sampling strategies and operational planning.

Crop Monitoring

Visual and data-driven field monitoring to identify variability, observe development patterns and improve situational awareness across agricultural parcels.

Early Detection Support

Aerial review of conditions that may indicate stress, irregular development or issues requiring agronomic follow-up and validation.

Irrigation & Resource Support

Aerial observation to help identify irregularities and support review of land behavior, irrigation patterns and field heterogeneity.

Livestock

Animal Location & Counting

Aerial support for locating animals, observing spatial distribution and assisting livestock monitoring over large or complex areas.

Grazing & Parcel Supervision

Drone observation of pasture use, land occupation, enclosure conditions and distribution patterns across livestock areas.

Infrastructure Review

Visual inspection of fences, water points, feeding zones and other operational infrastructure linked to livestock activities.

Behaviour Observation

Aerial support for observing movement patterns, grouping, dispersion and other visible dynamics relevant to field supervision.

Large-Area Monitoring

Rapid visual review of extensive rural areas for more agile operational oversight and situational awareness.

Digital Management Support

Drone-enabled workflows that integrate with broader digital systems for livestock and land management.

Our approach combines aerial capability, operational understanding and innovation to support more efficient, intelligent and scalable agricultural and livestock management.
